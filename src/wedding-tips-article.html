Wedding Invitations 2019 - The Trends

In love, engaged and Zack - time for wedding invitations! And so it's time for us to introduce you to the 2019 trends for your wedding invitation cards!

Unbelievable how the time goes by, when you have barely agreed on a date and you fall in love with the location, the invitation cards are ready.

And again it says: Who has the choice ... Because it is not so easy from all the formats, colors and motives to find the right one for themselves. Often a small look at the current trends helps. After all, it never hurts to be inspired.

That's why we've put together the 2019 Wedding Invitations for you.

Greenery wedding invitations

Green, green, green are all our cards. Well, not quite. And yet, wedding invitations featuring eucalyptus and other leafy greens are becoming increasingly popular. Especially in combination with fine gold accents and textured natural paper, the Greenery Look gets a special elegance.

Green, green, green are all our cards. Well, not quite. And yet, wedding invitations featuring eucalyptus and other leafy greens are becoming increasingly popular. Especially in combination with fine gold accents and textured natural paper, the Greenery Look gets a special elegance.

Greenery

Vintage for your wedding

Some may not even hear it (or in this case read) but the trend towards vintage wedding invitations will stay with us in 2019. Why not? Vintage weddings keep surprising us with their versatility. Whether the elegant Great Gatsby Party or (especially in trend) the rustic barn wedding - here is something for everyone!

For the vintage invitations for the wedding, especially motives with lace and wood are at the forefront. With additional leaves, tendrils and flowers, the Greenery theme can also be taken up in maps in wood look.

Also the popular Kraftkarton plays 2019 a large role. If you want an individual touch, you can also add some green to the invitation card after printing and attach dried jute flowers with a jute string.

Vintage

Wedding invitation cards in calligraphy style

At the latest since handlettering is on everyone's lips again, the calligraphy receives again on weddings. Sometimes the words are just the most important thing - just like your wedding invitation!

Since not everyone is blessed with a wonderful filigree handwriting, you will find in our range of elegant cards with restrained design. The wedding invitations put the curved font in the focus and c aptivate with a unique noble look.

Purple wedding cards

Who would have thought that. Now that Ultra Violet 2018 has been the Pantone Color of the Year, the trend is now reaching the wedding party and letting the 2019 wedding invitations shine in all sorts of purple shades.

Whether as a delicate color accent in lilac or with a strong violet as a wonderful contrast to the white wedding theme - with invitations in purple your 2019 is fully in vogue.

A wedding in berry tones

Whether bright colors for the summer wedding or muted tones for all autumn and winter weddings, with berry tones sets your wonderful splash of color. Especially the reduced color tones exude pure elegance and look particularly beautiful in combination with golden accents.

But even those who have fallen for the boho style, can fall back on the great color palette of berries. For example, strong raspberry tones are just perfect for the festival look or the laid-back garden wedding.

Berry

Golden elegance

Wedding invitations in classic and noble style are of course timeless. But gold will make it to a real leading position in 2019. Special finishes that give the invitation card that certain something and stylishly invite you to your big day, are a pretty eye-catcher in your wedding wallpaper.

Even soft gradients on high-quality shimmer paper simulate golden elegance and are also available for the smaller purse.

Golden

Pocketfold wedding invitations

As the only trend card format, pocketfold wedding invitation cards have made it into our 2019 ranking.

If you have a lot to say, you can use the folding cards with the possibility to put several depositors into a bag. Directions, daily routines, hotel information or reply cards find a nice place here without having to give up nice words for the actual invitation text.
